A brief message from us

Welcome to our cinematic bucket challenge!

We crafted this challenge to ignite the passion of aspiring cinephiles like you, making it easier and more enjoyable to explore the captivating world of film. We know you’re probably eager to begin your journey, but before you continue, we want to give you some tips and advice on how to use our site, and get the most enjoyment out of your experience.

When you look up recommended movies online, you will often stumble upon short, uninspired listicles over the same ten movie recommendations, or links to purchase books with titles such as “1000 films to see before you die!”. We don’t have a thousand movie recommendations for you yet, but what we do have is a fun, ever-evolving film bucket list for you to go through!

As these lists have a tendency to get very long and unwieldy, we have instead made our bucket challenge into smaller, bite-sized “mini-challenges”, each containing five films. We call these "Reels"! These films span many different time-periods and genres, and each selection is meant to give the viewer a broad taste of the many possibilities of the medium!

We sincerely hope you enjoy your journey - so make some popcorn, turn of the lights and let’s get into the first film!
